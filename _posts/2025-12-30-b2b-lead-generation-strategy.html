The three-layer system

  1. Authority: content, case studies, and proof
  2. Demand: search and LinkedIn distribution
  3. Conversion: landing pages and automation

Content that converts in AI search

  • Clear problem-to-solution framing
  • Specific outcomes and numbers
  • Single CTA with low friction

Paid distribution that scales

Target high-intent keywords and roles, then use retargeting to move prospects back to the offer.

Automation that protects pipeline

Use short nurture sequences, booked-call reminders, and lead scoring rules to keep leads warm.
