Crawl and index

  • Robots.txt is accurate
  • XML sitemap is clean
  • Canonicals are consistent
  • Important pages are indexed

Performance and UX

  • LCP under 2.5 seconds
  • CLS under 0.1
  • TTFB under 800ms
  • Images optimized

Architecture

  • Shallow click depth
  • Strong internal linking
  • No redirect chains

AI readiness

  • Schema markup validated
  • Author and date visible
  • Content structured with clear headings
