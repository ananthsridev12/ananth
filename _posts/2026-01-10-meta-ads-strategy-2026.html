The old playbook is gone

Pixel-only tracking under-reports results. You need first-party data and stronger measurement.

What works now

  1. CRM list uploads and lookalikes
  2. Conversions API alongside pixel
  3. Clear prospecting vs retargeting separation
  4. Creative testing every 2 to 3 weeks

Budget split I use

  • 70 percent prospecting
  • 20 percent retargeting
  • 10 percent testing

Measurement focus

Cost per qualified lead, time-to-conversion, and branded search lift matter more than platform-only ROAS.
