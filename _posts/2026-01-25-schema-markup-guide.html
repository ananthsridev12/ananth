Why schema matters for AI search

LLMs rely on structured data to understand entities. Schema improves machine readability and rich result eligibility.

High-impact schema types

  • Organization and Person
  • Article and BlogPosting
  • FAQ and HowTo
  • Breadcrumb
  • Service or Product

Implementation checklist

  1. Add Organization and Person on the homepage
  2. Use Article schema for blog posts
  3. Validate in Rich Results Test
  4. Monitor in Search Console

Common mistakes

  • Marking up hidden content
  • FAQ schema on thin pages
  • Missing author and publish dates

AI visibility tip

Schema does not replace content quality. It amplifies it. Pair it with a clear AEO structure.
