Attribution is about decision quality

The right model depends on your funnel maturity, conversion volume, and data cleanliness.

Quick model guide

  • First-click for early acquisition
  • Last-click for simplicity
  • Linear for balanced journeys
  • Time decay for recency-heavy funnels
  • Position-based for first/last emphasis
  • Data-driven when volume is high

My decision framework

  1. Early-stage: First-click or position-based
  2. Mid-stage: Linear or time decay
  3. Mature: Data-driven with stable tracking

AI-era pitfall

AI reporting can make weak data look confident. If tracking is inconsistent, use simpler models.

Recommendation

Lock the model for a quarter, then adjust based on business changes.
