What AEO means in 2026

Search is no longer just blue links. LLMs summarize, and your goal is to be the cited source behind the summary. That is AEO.

Signals that matter to AI answers

  • Direct, concise answers near the top
  • Clear entity relationships and author credibility
  • Structured data that exposes meaning
  • Topical depth across a cluster of pages
  • Freshness when topics change quickly

Content structure that LLMs parse well

  1. Answer first in 2 to 3 sentences
  2. Add context and constraints
  3. Provide steps or a checklist
  4. Back it with evidence or examples

Schema for AI visibility

I prioritize Organization, Person, Article, and FAQ schema because AI systems use those to understand entities and credibility.

Build authority with clusters

AEO works best when related posts link to each other. That is why I pair this with guides like Schema Markup and Technical SEO.

AEO checklist

  • Exact question phrasing in H2
  • Answer in the first 150 words
  • FAQ schema where it fits
  • Author and date clearly visible
