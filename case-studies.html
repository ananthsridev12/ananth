Ark Neo Financial Services (DhanLAP)

Scaling Lead Generation for Fintech Platform

40% Traffic Increase
25% Better Conversions
35% Lower CPL

Problem

DhanLAP needed to scale qualified lead generation for their financial services platform while maintaining cost efficiency. Existing campaigns had high CPL and poor conversion tracking.

Strategy & Execution

  • Implemented GA4, GTM, and attribution tracking for accurate data
  • Optimized landing pages with schema markup and structured data
  • Launched targeted Google Search campaigns for high-intent keywords
  • Integrated CRM with automation flows to improve MQL-to-SQL conversion
  • Built Looker Studio dashboards for real-time performance tracking

Measurable Results

  • Increased qualified traffic by 40% across all channels
  • Reduced CPL by 35% through campaign optimization
  • Improved lead-to-conversion rate by 25% via landing page optimization
  • Boosted organic visibility through consistent SEO efforts
  • Streamlined lead management with CRM automation
